Interests:

Other than anime I also enjoy delving into the field of pharmaceutical science . Pretty different huh hehe?

I've always had a strong interest in both healthcare and science, and pharmacy uniquely bridges these two passions.

My fascination began during a high school chemistry class where I was captivated by the complexities of chemical interactions and their potential to affect human health. This led me to volunteer at a local pharmacy, where I saw firsthand the pivotal role pharmacists play in patient care.

I was particularly inspired by the way the pharmacists not only dispensed medication but also offered advice and support to each patient, ensuring they felt informed and cared for.
What solidified my desire to pursue this career was the opportunity I had to assist in a community health campaign focused on diabetes management. It was there that I truly understood the impact of effective medication management and patient education. The chance to directly contribute to improving patients’ health outcomes is deeply rewarding to me.

I am eager to bring my dedication and passion for pharmacy to a role where I can continue making a tangible difference in patients’ lives.
